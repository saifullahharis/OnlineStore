Short Stories

A short story is a brief work of fiction that typically focuses on a single event or character. It is usually less than 10,000 words in length and can be read in one sitting.

Introduction

Brief explanation of the benefits of reading short stories: Reading short stories can improve reading comprehension, vocabulary, critical thinking skills, and provide entertainment.
